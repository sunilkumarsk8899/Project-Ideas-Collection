How to Use

  • Drag & Drop: Click and hold the `☰` icon to drag and reorder tasks. A notification will confirm the change.
  • Mark as Done: Use the toggle switch to mark tasks complete. This will strike-through the task and prevent further editing.
  • Edit Tasks: Click on any **time slot** or **activity** to edit the text (unless it's marked as done).
  • Add/Delete Rows: Use the `+ Add Row` button to add new tasks and the `×` button to remove them.
